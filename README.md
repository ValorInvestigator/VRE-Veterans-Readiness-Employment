## Key Regulations Every Veteran Should Know

- **38 CFR 21.53** — *"Any reasonable doubt shall be resolved in favor of a finding of feasibility"*
- **38 CFR 21.257(d)(3)** — VA pays for *"license or required fees"* under self-employment
- **38 CFR 21.362/21.364** — VA must follow a 3-step process before discontinuing you
- **38 CFR 21.420(e)** — Favorable findings are **binding** on subsequent adjudicators
- **38 USC 3104(a)(15)** — Services include "other appropriate services" (the catch-all)
